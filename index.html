About Xofee

Xofee began as a small idea between friends who simply loved good coffee and good conversation. In 2019, Shiva Chowdary decided to turn that idea into a real coffee shop, and he didn't do it alone — his close friends Damu Reddy and Sher joined him as business partners, each bringing their own strengths to the table.

While Shiva focused on sourcing quality beans and perfecting recipes, Damu Reddy built strong relationships with local coffee growers to keep every batch fresh, and Sher shaped the cozy, welcoming atmosphere that Xofee cafes are now known for. Together, the three of them turned a small corner shop into a name people trust for their daily cup.

Today, Xofee is more than just a coffee shop — it's a small community where students study, friends catch up, and strangers become regulars. And through all the growth, the goal has stayed exactly the same: every cup should tell a story.
